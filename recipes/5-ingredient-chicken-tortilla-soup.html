5 Ingredient Chicken Tortilla Soup

Original recipe crafted by The Modern Proper

Recipe First!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1 (19 oz) can red enchilada sauce
  • 1 (14 oz) can fire roasted tomatoes, diced
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweet corn, drained (or canned creamed corn)

Method

  1. Place all ingredients into a large soup pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Ladle soup into bowls and serve with your favorite fixings: lime juice, sour cream, shredded cheese, avocado, chips etc.

This soup is so easy to make, and you only need one pot! Hooray for fewer dirty dishes! It is inexpensive too - most grocery stores sell rotisserie chicken for $5-10, and the rest of the canned ingredients can be found for about $5 as well, bringing the cost per serving to $2.50 - $4.00.

I recommend also picking up some corn tortillas (like the kind used for street tacos). Crisp up the tortillas with some oil of your choice in a cast-iron skillet over medium heat. You just need enough oil to cover the bottom of your skillet. Put the crispy corn disk on top of your soup bowl and use your spoon to break it into pieces and enjoy!
